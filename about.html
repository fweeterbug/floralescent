How It All Started

Founded in 2010 by Josey, Angie, and Evie, Floralescent began as a modest floral shop in Meadowvale. With each passing year, their dedication to exquisite designs and exceptional service earned them a loyal following.

In 2015, their success led to a move into a larger, more spacious shop on the edge of town. This expansion not only accommodated their growing clientele but also opened doors to new opportunities.

Today, Floralescent stands as a symbol of community and creativity, inspiring all who step through its doors. Set in Downtown Royse City, Texas, they welcome everyone with open arms.

Their competitors such as, fromyouflowers, The Bouqs, & Farmgirl Flowers, will not deter them from their journey and growth.

The Founders

Evie

Growing up surrounded by hard workers on her family's ranch, Evie's childhood was full of leadership and dedication. Her fascination with flowers, nurtured from a young age, led her to explore the art of floral design, turning her passion into a profession. Now, as Floralescentl Co.'sProduct Manager, Evie combines her childhood wonder with strategic insight to curate enchanting floral experiences that delight customers and evoke the magic of her upbringing.

Josey

From her earliest memories among blooming gardens, Josey's hands were destined to weave floral magic. With a childhood steeped in the fragrance of petals and the dance of colors, she developed an innate talent for arranging blooms. Now, as Floralescent's Arrangement Specialist, Josey channels her lifelong passion into crafting enchanting bouquets that embody the essence of her floral-filled accomplishments.

Angelina

In the bustling hub of her childhood home, Angie's fascination with organization and efficiency took root. From color-coded schedules to meticulously kept records, she thrived in bringing order to chaos. Now, as Floralescent's Chief Secretary, Angie's cultivation shapes her dedication to keeping the administrative gears turning seamlessly, ensuring each client interaction is as smooth as the petals of a freshly bloomed flower.
